# Schema

Source columns include:
- `Name` (meteorite name)
- `ID` (source identifier)
- `Class` (meteorite class)
- `Mass_g` (grams, may be missing)
- `Year` (discovery or landing year, may need parsing to YYYY)
- `Lattitude` (latitude, numeric, may be missing)
- `Longitude` (longitude, numeric, may be missing)
- `Fall` (Fell or Found)
- `GeoLocation` (string coordinates)
- `Source_URL' (source of all data)
